Harry Potter and the cursed child

The legendary Harry Potter series

Harry Potter is a series of seven fantasy novels written by British author J. K. Rowling

These novels tell stories about the lives of a young wizard, Harry Potter, and his friends Hermione Granger and Ron Weasly at Hogwarts School of Wichcraft and Wizardry

The play inspired by a book

After the success of previous 7 books, J. K. Rowling did cooperate with John Tiffany and Jack Thorne to create the eighth story following up the journey of the wizards

The play "Harry Potter and the Cursed Child"

The play is divided into 2 parts, consisting of 2 acts each, and written by Jack Thorne. Previews of the play began at the Palace Theatre, London, on 7 June 2016, and it premiered on 30 July 2016

The speical thing about this play is: The play chronicle Harry Potter's son- Albus Severus. Instead of being assigns to Gryffindor like his parents, the Sorting Hat sorts him in Slytherin- the enemy of Gryffindor House. And since then, his amazing journey at Hogwart starts.
